What is Digestzymes?

Digestzymes is one capsule that does two things at once, and the second one is what makes it stand out. Most enzyme products just give you enzymes; this one gives you enzymes plus stomach acid. The acid comes from betaine HCl, which restores your stomach's natural acidity and starts protein digestion — and that is more useful than it sounds, because a surprising number of people with sluggish digestion are actually low on stomach acid, not high. Then come the enzymes: protease for protein, lipase for fat (this is the one that helps when a rich or greasy meal sits like a brick), and amylase for starch. There is also an enzyme with 'DPP-IV activity' that helps break down the gluten and casein pieces that bother people with a mild gluten or dairy sensitivity, plus lactase and a few others so it covers more of your plate. You take it right as you begin eating — one capsule, or two for a bigger meal — so the acid and enzymes are already in place when the food arrives. It is made by Designs for Health, a practitioner brand from Connecticut you mostly get through a clinician or their own store. The exact amounts are on your label, so check your bottle.
